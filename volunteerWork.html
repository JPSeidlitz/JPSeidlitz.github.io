    Summer Camps

    In the summer of 2024, I assisted with teaching a programming and robotics summer camp at Nolan Catholic High School for middle schoolers. The camp focused on teaching students the fundamentals of design and programming by building Lego robots to wrestle each other in a ring. While I primarily assisted students with their individual code, I did teach a few lessons myself. In addition to this camp, in 2023 I assisted Saint Andrew’s middle school with their performance of ByeBye Birdie by teaching them how to work with lighting systems.

    Audio at Mass

    Almost every week for two years, I ran the sound system for my school’s weekly Catholic Mass. This involved not only putting the mic on the priest, but also managing the audio channels from the choir and lecterns. A major issue I had to deal with was the tendency for the mics to pick up feedback due to the room Mass was being held being made mostly of concrete, thus echoing sound very well. Additionally, I ran the sound system for various school events such as assemblies, school wide presentations, and guest speakers.
